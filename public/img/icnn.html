Indian Chemical News

Client Profile

Indian Chemical News aspires to be the most respected and comprehensive provider of information for well defined niche groups in the Chemical and Petrochemical businesses. It is an important online resource for news, views, analysis, trends, technology updates and interviews with prominent leaders in the chemical and petrochemical space.

The aim of www.indianchemicalnews.com is to provide independent, authoritative and interactive chemical and petrochemical information, enabling their customers to make better-informed business and planning decisions. Their mission is to be India's most credible information portal for the chemical, petrochemical and allied industries.

Objective

The core objective was to provide a user-friendly website to add and maintain a database that includes news, views, analysis, trends, technology updates and interviews with prominent leaders in the chemical and petrochemical space. The client wanted Team Ezbiz to redesign and redevelop the website as the old version of the website was full of technical glitches and poor in terms of UI/UX and speed.

This purpose was to create a website where the admin can list the latest news, trends, interviews and e- conferences of the Chemical & Petrochemical sectors.

Services Provided

  • Design Prototype
  • Frontend Development
  • Backend Development
  • QA & testing
  • Digital Marketing

Approach

We suggested customised solution for developing a website leveraging the power of HTML and PHP. The web experts came up with a strategic architecture in one go that fulfills the project objectives. The team kept the navigation simple on the homepage that guides visitors to explore all the news categories and e- conferences where people can register themselves to attend the webinar and we are maintaining the database simultaneously. The data migration was planned and put in the place effectively with no flaws at all.

The website development and testing were put in sync to avoid any iterations at the end of the project to save time & to ensure quality and flawless delivery of this website.

Results

The client was delighted with the design, state of art UI/UX, features and development efforts of Ezbiz team.
